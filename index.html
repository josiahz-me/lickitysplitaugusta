About Us

We are a small ice cream shop located in Augusta, Michigan, right off of M-96. With years of serving our community under our belt, we have a reputation for quality and commitment to customer service. Serving everything from Hot Dogs to Sno Cones, we pride ourselves on being a place where everyone can find something yummy to eat!
